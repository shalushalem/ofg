Privacy Policy

OFG Connects  ·  Effective: June 24, 2025  ·  Last Updated: June 24, 2025

Welcome to OFG Connects — a Christian media streaming platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information.

1. Introduction

OFG Connects ("OFG", "we", "us") is a platform for pastors, worship leaders, Bible teachers, and ministry creators. By using OFG Connects, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account: Name, email address, password (hashed — never stored in plain text)
  • Profile: Display name, handle, bio, optional avatar
  • Content: Videos and descriptions you upload
  • Donations: Amount and optional message. Payment details processed by Razorpay — we never store card/UPI details

Automatically Collected

  • Usage: Videos watched, duration, likes, saves, shares
  • Device: Type, OS, app version
  • Recommendation signals: Watch completion, category preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver personalized content recommendations
  • Process donations between users and creators
  • Send relevant notifications (donations, comments, follows)
  • Maintain platform safety and prevent fraud
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Comply with applicable laws

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your data. We share only with:

  • Creators: Your name if you donate publicly; "Anonymous Supporter" if anonymous
  • Razorpay: For payment processing (subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare: Video/media storage via Cloudflare R2
  • Legal: If required by law or court order

5. Data Security

  • Salted SHA-256 password hashing
  • HTTPS for all data in transit
  • Encrypted secure token storage on device
  • Regular security reviews

In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active. After account deletion, most data is removed within 30 days. Donation transaction records are retained for 7 years for legal compliance.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request account deletion (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

Email: ofgtechhub@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

OFG Connects is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes via in-app notification.
